Aldridge Traffic Systems 8" "Durasig" halogen

Manufactured: Late 1970's & 1980's

One of the 3 oldest styles of ATS incandescent lights. These are made from plastic, and look identical to Durasig traffic lights, which I believe are the predecessor to these lights. The lenses have the 'ATS' logo embossed in the centre.

Looking from the front, these look identical to the other 3 ATS light designs from this era.
I have not been able to find any examples of a 12" variant.
