Aldridge Traffic Systems 8" "ATS Sydney" halogen

Manufactured: Late 1970's & 1980's

One of the 3 oldest styles of ATS incandescent lights. These are made from plastic, and look identical to a variant of AWA halogen lights (other than branding, of course). Not sure if AWA copied the design off of ATS, or the other way around.
The lenses have the 'ATS' logo embossed in the centre.

Looking from the front, these look identical to the other 3 ATS light designs from this era.
These lenses are also what AWA used on most of their polycarbonate lights.
