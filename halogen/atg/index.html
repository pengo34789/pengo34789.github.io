Aldridge Traffic Group 8" halogen

Manufactured: Early 2010's

The final revision of halogen signals: Same design as as before, other than a branding change, of course.

The front has the 'Aldridge Traffic Group' logo moulded onto it, however the 'Aldridge Traffic Systems' branding remains on the back.

While I presume 12" variants of these lights were made, I haven't been able to find any examples of them due to the scarce usage of halogen lights in the 2010's, with the usage of 12" lights being even rarer.
