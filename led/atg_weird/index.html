Aldridge Traffic Systems ? LED signals

Manufactured: Early-mid 2010's

An uncommon version of 8" Aldridge LED light (Unsure if 12" lights exist.) These have Aldridge Traffic Group branding on the front, however the Aldridge Traffic Systems branding remains on the back.

The lenses look similar to those of later Aldridge halogen lights, and arrow lights of this variant have a very narrow arrow shape. I have only been able to find arrow and ball signal variants.
