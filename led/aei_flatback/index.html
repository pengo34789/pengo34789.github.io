Aldridge Electrical Industries LED signals

Manufactured: Early-mid 2010's

An uncommon version of 8" Aldridge LED light (Unsure if 12" lights exist.) These have a little 'E' on the front for branding, and the front door is held on with a hinge and plastic clips. The back has AEI branding on it, and is relatively smooth and rounded.

I believe the fronts are copied from the Bob Panich light design, a company that Aldridge bought out in 2012. I have only been able to find arrow, ball, and pedestrian signal variants.
