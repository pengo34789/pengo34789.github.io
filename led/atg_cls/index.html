Aldridge Traffic Group 'CLS' LED signals

Manufactured: Mid 2010's - present

A light design that is becoming more common in Victoria, and has a more even light output compared to other Aldridge LED designs. These are similar to the original Tyco LED design, so it is hard to discern between them and CLS lights, especially in retrofits.

A rough guide to identifying them is that CLS modules are generally more shiny, the green of CLS modules is more cool compared to Tycos, and Aldridge's white CLS modules do not have the CLS lens design - The white module's lens look more akin to the ATS LED pedestrian light lens design.
