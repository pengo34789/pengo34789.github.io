Aldridge Traffic Group LED signal - Other revision

Manufactured: Early-mid 2010's

An unique version of an ATS signal - The back housing is very similar to an Aldridge Electrical Industries light (with vertical ridges,) however it and the front have normal Aldridge Traffic Group branding.

The only location that this has been found at is at the Anakie Rd railway crossing, in Bell Park, Geelong, VIC.
